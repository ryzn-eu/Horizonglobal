Our Background

A foundation built on problem-solving.

Founded by Sebastian Brotto as a modest tech initiative, Horizon Global began with a simple goal: to improve the efficiency of local data and energy systems. By focusing on practical problem-solving, our early software frameworks gradually expanded into broader utility management.

Today, we maintain that same foundational approach. We have grown to encompass multiple disciplines, but our core mission remains unchanged: delivering reliable, accessible services that help communities function smoothly and sustainably.

Our Services.

A balanced portfolio focused on renewable energy, digital connectivity, and accessible media experiences.

IREO

Energy & Utilities

IREO is dedicated to sustainable utility management. We focus on delivering renewable electricity, supporting water purification projects, and maintaining reliable infrastructure for the communities we serve in Europe.

Aura Ecosystem

Media Platform

Aura is our dedicated music and social platform. Created alongside Ryzn Music Group, it aims to connect artists directly with listeners, providing clear audio quality and a straightforward, fair ecosystem for creators.

Net-Orion

Telecommunications

Net-Orion provides the foundational telecommunications networks for our services. By maintaining reliable fiber optics and data centers, we ensure stable connectivity for both our internal operations and our regional customers.

Dev.Ryzn

Software Development

Dev.Ryzn is our core software team. They build the applications that manage our utility grids and power our digital platforms, focusing on security, stability, and practical AI tools to improve urban logistics.

MoonLight

MoonLight Production is our creative storytelling division. We focus on supporting independent voices and producing thoughtful, engaging narratives for a wide audience.
